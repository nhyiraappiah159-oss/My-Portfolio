I am a detail-obsessed Junior Web Developer and UI/UX Designer who turns complex ideas into clean, accessible and high-converting digital products. Over the past year I have shipped responsive websites, design systems and mobile-first interfaces for clients and institutions across Ghana — blending sharp visual design with solid front-end engineering in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and Figma.

From user research and wireframes to pixel-perfect, performant code, I own the full journey and obsess over loading speed, accessibility (WCAG), and the small interactions that make a product feel premium.
